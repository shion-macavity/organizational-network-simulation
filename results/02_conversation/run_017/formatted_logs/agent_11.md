3.  **Community Development:** Attract new survivors to your settlement, manage their needs, assign roles (engineers, doctors, farmers, scouts), and keep morale high. Each survivor could have unique skills and stories.
4.  **Cooperative Global Map:** Players from different settlements (either AI or other players) operate on a shared, slowly expanding world map. They can trade resources, send expeditions to help each other, or collaborate on large-scale reconstruction projects.
5.  **Environmental Challenges:** Dynamic weather systems, mutated wildlife, or localized atmospheric anomalies that require strategic planning and adaptation.
6.  **Mystery & Exploration:** Uncover clues about "The Great Silence" through exploration, ancient data logs, and ruins. This could involve puzzle-solving elements.

**Slogan:** "Rebuild. Reconnect. Remember."
