    *   **Daily Routine System:** Go to your mundane job (e.g., librarian, gardener, barista), run errands, attend neighborhood meetings.
    *   **Subtle Power Usage:** Encounter small-scale problems that can be solved with your powers discreetly. Failure to be subtle could lead to suspicion.
    *   **"Maintaining Secrecy" Mini-games:** Quick-time events or puzzle sequences to avoid revealing your identity during close calls.
    *   **Community Building:** Earn trust and friendship from your neighbors. Maybe there are other retired heroes nearby, leading to small co-op challenges or shared nostalgia.
    *   **Emergent Narrative:** Random events could trigger, like a local cat stuck in a tree, or a minor bank robbery where you have to decide if and how to intervene without blowing your cover.
