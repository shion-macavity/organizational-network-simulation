**Key Selling Points:**
1.  **Unique Genre Blend:** Cozy life-sim meets cosmic horror with a dash of mystery.
2.  **Unpredictable & Deep Characters:** Every guest is a unique, potentially world-ending challenge.
3.  **Humorous & Tense:** The constant tension of managing divine beings, balanced with charming innkeeping.
