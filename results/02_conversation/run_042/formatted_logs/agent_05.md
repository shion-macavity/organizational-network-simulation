1.  **Concept:** What's the core idea? The elevator pitch?

3.  **Contents:** What will the player *do*? What are the main mechanics, features, and activities? This is where the gameplay style comes in.
4.  **Slogan:** A catchy phrase that encapsulates the game's essence.
