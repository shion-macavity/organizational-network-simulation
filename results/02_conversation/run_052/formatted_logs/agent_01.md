The gameplay could be a blend of cooperative survival, base building, and a kind of strategic "terraforming" or environmental puzzle-solving. Players would form small communes or exploration teams, venturing into hazardous, overgrown, or desiccated landscapes. Do you use potentially dangerous ancient tech for rapid progress, or stick to safer, slower methods?
