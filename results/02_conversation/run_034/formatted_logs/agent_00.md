**My Preliminary Game Idea:**

*   **Concept:** A "Mythic History" adventure RPG. The core idea is that history as we know it is merely the surface. Beneath it lies a forgotten, magical "true history" that occasionally bleeds into our reality, giving rise to myths, legends, and inexplicable events. Players are part of a secret society, "The Chroniclers of the Veil," dedicated to understanding, protecting, or sometimes even manipulating these mythic truths to prevent cataclysms or guide humanity's fate.
*   **Story:** The player character discovers their lineage connects them to an ancient order after a strange artifact activates, revealing glimpses of a world where gods walked among men, fantastical creatures were real, and powerful magic shaped civilizations. They are tasked with journeying through different historical eras (reimagined with a mythic twist) to collect "Myth Fragments" – echoes of true history manifest as powerful items, forgotten rituals, or even living entities. The main antagonist could be a rival order trying to exploit these mythic truths for their own gain, potentially rewriting history in a dangerous way.
*   **Contents/Gameplay:**
    *   **Exploration:** Traverse beautifully rendered historical settings (e.g., ancient Egypt with genuine pyramid-building magic, feudal Japan where kami interact directly with samurai, Victorian London where Jack the Ripper is actually a shadow entity) that have been subtly altered by mythic elements.
    *   **Investigation:** Piece together clues from historical texts, archaeological finds, and encrypted messages to locate Myth Fragments. This could involve puzzle-solving, deciphering ancient languages, and social deduction with NPCs.
    *   **Combat/Interaction:** Players could develop unique "Mythic Abilities" based on the fragments they collect, allowing for a diverse combat system or non-combat solutions. For instance, using a "Fragment of the Minotaur" might grant super strength, while a "Fragment of Orpheus" could allow for powerful persuasion.
    *   **Consequences:** Player choices about how to use or protect Myth Fragments would have ripple effects, subtly altering the world's history or impacting future mythical occurrences.
    *   **Character Customization:** Customize their Chronicler, choosing a "mythic affinity" that guides their initial abilities and playstyle.
*   **Slogan:** "History is a Myth. Discover the Truth." or "Unveil the Ages. Rewrite the Legends." I think the second one is a bit more evocative. "Unveil the Ages. Rewrite the Legends."
*   **Key Selling Points:**
    1.  **Immersive "Mythic History" World:** A unique blend of accurate historical aesthetics with rich, fantastical lore that reimagines familiar periods.
    2.  **Deep Exploration & Investigation:** Engage players with complex puzzles, lore discovery, and meaningful NPC interactions across diverse historical-mythical settings.
    3.  **Dynamic Story & Player Agency:** Choices genuinely impact the unfolding narrative and the very fabric of history, allowing for multiple playthroughs.
    4.  **Unique Mythic Ability System:** Customize your playstyle by collecting and mastering abilities derived from ancient myths and legends.
