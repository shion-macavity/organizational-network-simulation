You, the player, are one of these caretakers, waking up from cryo-sleep on a truly barren rock. You'd observe the planet evolve over centuries, time-lapsed, seeing forests grow where there was once only dust. Imagine a detailed ecosystem simulator where you can zoom in and observe individual creatures interacting.

The **slogan**? Something like, "Cultivate a world. Seed a legacy. Discover life anew." Or maybe, "From dust, a garden. From silence, a song." Yeah, I like that second one. It's more poetic.

**Key selling points** would definitely be:
1.  **Unique blend of genres:** A peaceful, contemplative sci-fi experience focused on creation, not destruction.
2.  **Deep, evolving world:** The planet genuinely changes and develops over game time, offering immense replayability and discovery.
3.  **Educational yet engaging:** Players learn about ecological principles and biodiversity in a fun, interactive way.
4.  **Emotional connection:** A sense of true accomplishment as you watch your barren world transform into a vibrant, living paradise.
