*   **Environmental Puzzles:** The spreading digital jungle creates obstacles and new pathways. Players have to solve puzzles by manipulating the digital environment – redirecting data flows, pruning overgrown pathways, or even creating specific biomes to attract certain AI sprites.
*   **Narrative Choices:** Kael's choices impact the story and the city. Do you spread the digital wilderness rapidly, risking system-wide collapse, or do you carefully nurture it, seeking harmony? The city's authoritarian overseers become aware of the anomaly, and you'll have to evade or outsmart them.
*   **Customization:** Personalize your digital garden, designing unique species and environments.

**Slogan:** "Unearth the wild. Re-code humanity."

1.  **Unique Genre Blend:** A fresh take on sci-fi combining intricate environmental puzzles, emergent AI life simulation, and a compelling narrative-driven mystery.

3.  **Meaningful Player Choice:** Your actions directly influence the evolution of the digital ecosystem and the fate of humanity's future, leading to multiple endings.
