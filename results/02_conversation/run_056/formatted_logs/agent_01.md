    *   **Key Selling Points:**
        1.  Unique blend of cooperative strategy, life-sim, and environmental narrative.
        2.  Focus on hope and reconstruction rather than constant conflict.
        3.  Rich world-building with deep lore of a post-war Earth.
        4.  Meaningful player choices that impact a living, evolving world.
