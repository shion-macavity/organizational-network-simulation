An RPG structure is almost a given for story, but what about a puzzle element for technological challenges? Perhaps a deep, player-driven narrative where choices genuinely alter the future of a galactic civilization, or a game that blends exploration with a compelling ethical framework. Or a game that makes you *feel* the vastness and loneliness of space, not just as a backdrop, but as a core emotional driver.

A lone explorer, a member of a resistance, an AI with a conscience? I want to think about the "what if" at the heart of the story. 5.  **Contents & Slogan Keywords:** As I'm brainstorming, I'll jot down keywords for potential content – types of planets, alien species, technologies, social structures, unique gameplay mechanics.
