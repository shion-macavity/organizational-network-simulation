2.  **Story Hook:** What immediately draws a player in?
3.  **Key Gameplay Loop:** What do players actually *do* most of the time?
4.  **Unique Selling Points:** What makes this game stand out?
5.  **Target Audience:** Who are we making this for?
6.  **Slogan Ideas:** Short, punchy phrases that embody the game.
