## ■ ターン 0: 初期状態
* **【初期思考】**
> Alright, new project! My department, Slice of Life Game Planning, is tasked with coming up with a new game. The cool thing is, it's pretty open-ended, not just strictly "slice of life" but we can bring in anything. Action, RPG, puzzles... doesn't matter. Gotta think outside the box, they said. That's a good challenge for me, Ito, at 43, keeping things balanced between creative and practical.

So, the first step is to just generate some core ideas: story, concept, contents, slogan, and key selling points. No need to worry about the nitty-gritty tech stuff yet, just the essence of the game.

Okay, "slice of life" usually means everyday stuff, personal growth, relationships, maybe a bit of a gentle pace. How can I twist that?
What if we take the mundane and put it in an extraordinary setting? Or vice-versa?

Here's an idea popping into my head:
Let's call it **"Echoes of the Forgotten Grove."**

**Concept:** It's a life simulation game, but with a unique twist. You're not just building a farm or a town; you're restoring a mystical forest grove and helping its long-lost, unique inhabitants remember their past lives and rediscover their purpose. It combines elements of farming/resource management, narrative-driven exploration, and a deep, emotional connection system.

**Story:** The player character is drawn to a secluded, overgrown grove that mysteriously appeared on the edge of a forgotten realm. Upon entering, they discover it's not empty. Faint, ethereal beings, "Echoes," reside there, unable to remember who they are or what they once did. These Echoes are manifestations of past lives – maybe a legendary chef, a wise storyteller, a brave guardian, or a clumsy inventor. The grove itself is also fading. Your job is to cultivate the land, grow unique crops, craft items, and perform various activities to slowly bring memories back to the Echoes and restore vitality to the grove. As you help them, their true forms and personalities emerge, unlocking new abilities for them to help you, and revealing the mystery of why the grove and its inhabitants were forgotten.

**Contents:**
*   **Grove Cultivation:** Farming unique, glowing plants and fungi that have specific magical properties. Resource gathering from the environment.
*   **Echo Interaction:** Deep dialogue system where choices influence an Echo's memory recovery. Each Echo has a unique personality and story arc. You might need to cook specific dishes for the chef Echo, find lost artifacts for the guardian Echo, or help the inventor Echo gather materials for forgotten contraptions.
*   **Memory Restoration Mini-games:** Small puzzle or activity segments related to an Echo's past life. For example, recreating a melody for a musician Echo, or solving a riddle for a scholar Echo.
*   **Crafting and Building:** Using collected resources to craft tools, build structures (like a communal kitchen, library, or workshop) to aid the Echoes, and customize your own small living space within the grove.
*   **Exploration:** The grove itself expands as you restore it, revealing new biomes, hidden areas, and perhaps other forgotten realms or ruins.
*   **Relationship System:** Deepen bonds with the Echoes. As their memories return, they become more vibrant, and their "friendship level" unlocks new lore, abilities, and even special events. Maybe you can even help them achieve a final "rest" or choose to have them stay and rebuild the grove with you.
*   **Day/Night Cycle & Seasons:** Influences plant growth, Echo behavior, and available resources. Different events might happen at specific times.

**Slogan:** "Unearth Memories. Cultivate Wonders. Rekindle Forgotten Souls."

**Key Selling Points:**
1.  **Unique Emotional Narrative:** A deeply touching story about identity, loss, and the power of memory, presented in a gentle, contemplative way.
2.  **Harmonious Blend of Genres:** Combines the relaxing loop of life-sim/farming with meaningful storytelling, light exploration, and puzzle-solving.
3.  **Vibrant, Memorable Characters:** The "Echoes" are not just NPCs; they are the heart of the game, each with a rich, unfolding personal history and a distinct personality.
4.  **Sense of Purpose and Growth:** Players experience a real sense of impact as they visibly restore the grove and help the Echoes reclaim their past, fostering a unique connection to the game world.

Okay, that's a solid initial idea, I think. It hits the "slice of life" emotional and relational aspects, but in a very fantastical, "outside the box" way.
