*   **Customization:** Deep customization for player avatars, their workshop spaces, and the visual flair of their creations.
*   **Audience Interaction/Judging:** The audience (NPCs or other players in multiplayer) provides dynamic feedback and scores based on speed, precision, creativity, and overall spectacle. This could involve mini-game elements or quick-time events to "wow" the crowd.
*   **Resource Management:** Gather materials, upgrade tools, manage reputation.
