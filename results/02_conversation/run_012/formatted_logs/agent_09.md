It's about interconnectedness and the beauty of creation.

*   **Exploration:** Navigating through nebulae, discovering new celestial bodies with unique conditions.
*   **Terraforming/Astro-forming mechanics:** Manipulating atmospheric conditions, introducing water, creating energy sources, seeding microbial life, and then guiding its evolution over eons within the game.
*   **Ecosystem management:** Balancing predator-prey relationships, ensuring biodiversity, dealing with cosmic events like supernovas or black holes that could disrupt your carefully cultivated worlds.
*   **Technological progression:** Researching new seeding methods, genetic manipulation tools, or cosmic survey equipment.
*   **Narrative layers:** Discovering ancient ruins, messages from previous cosmic gardeners, or encountering other nascent civilizations you might choose to nurture or observe from afar.

The **slogan** could be something like: "Cultivate the cosmos. Witness life's grand design." Or perhaps, "From dust to dawn: Your universe awaits."

1.  **Unique blend of Sci-Fi and meditative life simulation:** A refreshing take on both genres, focusing on creation rather than destruction.
2.  **Vast, procedurally generated cosmic sandbox:** Every playthrough offers new systems and challenges to nurture.
3.  **Deep, evolving ecosystems:** See the direct impact of your choices over cosmic timescales, watching life adapt and thrive.
4.  **Relaxing yet strategic gameplay:** Perfect for players who enjoy thoughtful decision-making and a sense of long-term accomplishment, without constant pressure.
