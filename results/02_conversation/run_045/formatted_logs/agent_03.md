Focus on exploration, crafting, community building.

**Story idea:** You're part of a small, isolated settlement. A mysterious illness starts affecting people, and the only possible cure is rumoured to be found in the ruins of a pre-Unplugging megacity, deep in what's now a dangerous, overgrown wilderness. But the journey isn't just about finding a cure; it's about piecing together why the Unplugging happened and what humanity lost – and gained. Maybe the "cure" isn't what they think it is, and the old tech was causing the illness? Or maybe there's a different way to live.

**Contents:**
*   **Exploration:** Vast, beautiful, overgrown ruins of cities, forests reclaiming highways, flooded subway tunnels. Discover hidden caches, old data terminals (that might need to be hand-cranked or solar-powered in a rudimentary way to access fragments of information).
*   **Crafting & Engineering:** Use old car parts to build a bicycle, salvage electronics to create simple radios or light sources, use building materials from fallen skyscrapers to reinforce your settlement. Resource management would be key. Maybe even creating your own water wheels or wind turbines for local, limited power.
*   **Community Building:** As you explore, you find other survivors, some friendly, some hostile. You can recruit them to your settlement, assign them tasks, watch your community grow and develop. Each NPC could have unique skills.
*   **Wildlife:** Nature has reclaimed much. New species might have emerged due to ecological shifts, or old animals have simply thrived. Not necessarily "mutated monsters," but just wild, dangerous animals you need to hunt or avoid.
*   **Faction System:** Different groups of survivors with different ideologies – those who want to rebuild the old world, those who embrace the new simplicity, and those who are just bandits. Your choices influence who you ally with.

**Slogan:** "The World Went Silent. Now, We Build A Song." or maybe "The Past Is Gone. The Future Is Ours To Build." I like "The World Went Silent. Now, We Build A Song." It implies hope and creativity.

**Key Selling Points:**
1.  **Unique Post-Apocalyptic Vision:** No zombies, no nuclear winter. A beautiful, serene, yet challenging world where nature has reclaimed human structures.
2.  **Empowering Crafting & Rebuilding Loop:** Go beyond mere survival; actively repurpose discarded technology and build a thriving community from the ground up.
3.  **Deep Exploration with Narrative Twists:** Uncover the true story of "The Great Unplugging" through environmental storytelling and intriguing quests.
4.  **Meaningful Player Choice:** Shape your community and determine the future of humanity through your actions and alliances.
