1.  **Unique Perspective:** Shifting focus from combat to consequences and reconstruction.
2.  **Deep Strategic & Moral Choices:** Players face complex dilemmas with no easy answers.
3.  **Human-Centric Narrative:** Emphasizes the resilience and struggles of people in post-conflict scenarios.
4.  **Challenging & Rewarding Gameplay:** Success isn't about crushing enemies, but about fostering lasting peace and prosperity.
